About Our Saloon

Welcome to the Ripped Donkey Saloon! We are a historic old west style saloon serving up cold drinks and good times in Anytown since 1889. Stop by our rustic wooden bar for a craft beer or whiskey, then grab a table to enjoy live music and watch the game.

Food & Drinks Menu

View our menu featuring pub fare, burgers, wings and more. We also offer a great selection of beers on tap, classic cocktails and a whiskey list with over 50 options.
